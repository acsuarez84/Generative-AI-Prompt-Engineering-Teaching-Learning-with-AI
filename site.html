A. C. Suarez — AI & Writing Pedagogy


Welcome

This site is a collection of projects by A. C. Suarez focused on generative AI, prompt engineering, and writing pedagogy. These resources are designed for multilingual learners, writing instructors, and curriculum designers who want to use AI thoughtfully in educational contexts.

Projects

  • The Analytical Map

    An interactive Socratic brainstorming tool that guides writers through six analytical lenses — Issue, Time, Language, Rhetoric, Author, and Genre — to deepen engagement with any academic text before forming an argument.

  • AI & Writing Pedagogy Hub

    A resource portal covering the philosophy behind these tools, pedagogical rationale, and guidance for writing instructors and curriculum designers integrating AI into their practice.

  • Source Code Viewer

    A syntax-highlighted browser for exploring the HTML, CSS, and JavaScript behind The Analytical Map — for educators who want to understand, adapt, or remix the tool.

  • UCF AI Presentation (PDF)

    A slide deck developed for the University of Central Florida covering generative AI in teaching and learning, prompt-engineering strategies, classroom applications, and ethical considerations.

About

These tools address a core challenge in writing pedagogy: helping multilingual writers engage more deeply with academic texts through guided Socratic reasoning rather than surface-level summarising. Rather than asking AI to generate answers, students learn to use AI as a structured thinking partner — working through a text systematically before forming their own arguments.

All resources run entirely in the browser with no login, no data collection, and no server required. They are freely available to use, fork, and adapt.
